Roasted Potatoes Recipe

Description

You can't go wrong with these oven-roasted potatoes! They're simple and delicious, and they'll have you dreaming of Nonna's Sunday lunches. They're crispy, golden, and delicious-the potatoes that make your tastebuds take a trip back to your childhood.

You don't need special equipment or ingredientsâ€”grab a few potatoes and prepare for some serious flavour. All you have to do is pop them in the oven and wait for that delicious smell to wave through your house. Furthermore, they're so easy to make that even if you're new to cooking, we promise you'll still end up with perfectly cooked potatoes every time!

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp salt
  • Rosemary stalks
  • Garlic
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • water
  • 6 large potatoes

Steps

  1. To make the roasted potatoes, peel the skin off and cut them into your preferred size, placing them in the bowl of water once cut.
  2. Then, rinse and drain the potatoes once again and put them into a clean bowl.
  3. Sprinkle a few pinches of fine salt over the potatoes, rubbing it over them really well, mixing it through using your hands.
  4. Transfer the potatoes into an oven safe baking tray and spread them out, before adding a few short rosemary stalks and garlic cloves (with the skin still on, making a small slit with a knife).
  5. Braise the roasted potatoes at a medium heat on the stove top, mixing and turning them with a fork every so often, for up to 15 minutes.
  6. Place the braised potatoes in the oven to cook for up to 30 minutes or until they are crispy and golden.
  7. Once the roasted potatoes are cooked, remove them from the oven.
