Cheesy Garlic Bread Recipe

The everyday version of the outrageous Cheese and Garlic Crack Bread, this is the perfect shape for dunking into soups and stews, or serve with a salad to make a meal. Make it once and you'll memorise it!

This is a recipe/method that everyone should memorise and use forever. You don't need to use my exact quantities, make this your own! Add herbs, parmesan, more garlic, less garlic, chilli 😳, truffle 😱, pesto 😍.

Take soup from great to OMG I could have this every day!! Take a stew from mmmmm to please make this again tomorrow!!! Serve this with a salad to make a meal. Or …. you know. Just eat it, as is.

Ingredients

  • 1 bread loaf approx 30cm/12" long
  • 100 g/0.5 cup unsalted butter
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 3 garlic cloves (minced 3 tsp)
  • 2-2.5 cups (200 - 250g) mozzarella cheese, grated
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 160C/320F.
  2. Mix butter with garlic and salt.
  3. Cut bread in half horizontally.
  4. Drizzle with garlic butter all over-don't miss any patches!
  5. Bake 20 minutes until bread is crispy on the edges.
  6. Crank oven up to 200C/390F. Top with cheese, bake 7 - 10 min until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Sprinkle with parsley if using, cut bread into pieces. Serve immediately as a side for meals, dunking in soups and stews!
